    Khichan Bird Sanctuary

    Bird Watching Holiday in Khichan


    Khichan has a cluster of ponds (talao) that typically host large variety of birds, especially
    waders, making it a birder’s paradise. You can mark the village by a shawl of grey
    fluttering over the amber dusk in the winters. Khichan has a predominantly Jain population.
